Research :

• Hybrid Mode Motion Planning for Underactuated System with Contact Constraint

The generation of optimal trajectories for high-dimensional robotic systems under constraints remains computationally challenging due to the need to simultaneously satisfy dynamic feasibility, input limits, and task-specific objectives while searching over high-dimensional spaces. This paper generalizes the AGHF formulation to accommodate arbitrary cost functions, significantly expanding the classes of trajectories that can be generated. It also introduces Phase1 - Phase 2 Algorithm that enables the use of constraint-violating initial guesses while guaranteeing satisfactory convergence.

Projects :

• Trajectory Optimization and Nonlinear MPC for Planar Biped using Contact-Constrained Dynamics

This project investigates agile gait generation and real-time tracking control for a planar 5-link biped using optimization-based control. The central objective is to assess whether a two-stage 'offline trajectory optimization + online nonlinear MPC' pipeline can enable robust and agile locomotion behaviors under disturbances.

Special thank to Professor Yanran Ding. This was a final project of his course legged robot control.

Github repo with demo : Convex-Optimization-solver

• Arbitrary Dimensional Linear Optimization Solver With Interior Point Method

This article focuses on solving aribitray dimensional linear programming. I designed and implemented a numerical linear program solver that can be applied to arbitrary dimension. This solver can be extended to solving an arbitrary convex problem with little effort by replacing analytical derivatives with numerical derivatives.

The process of the solver taking newton steps, along with both inequality constraints (red) and equality constraints (grey).

Special thank to Professor Dmitry Berenson. This was a final project of his robotics course.
