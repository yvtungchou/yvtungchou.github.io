• Arbitrary Dimensional Linear Optimization Solver

This article focuses on solving a subclass of convex optimization problem called linear program, where the objective function and constraint functions are all linear. I designed and implemented a numerical linear program solver that can be applied to arbitrary dimension. This solver can be extended to solving an arbitrary convex problem with little effort by replacing analytical derivatives with numerical derivatives.

An illustration of the solving process, where there is a linear objective function, along with both inequality constraints (red) and equality constraints (grey).
